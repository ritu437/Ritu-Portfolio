Hi, I’m Ritu!

Currently a Master’s student in Carnegie Mellon University’s Human Computer Interaction Institute, I come from a background of mechanical engineering and worked as a process engineer after undergrad.

I’m really excited by upcoming technologies’ potential for impact and influence in society, particularly in the context of human relationships. I think that if well utilized, many of these technologies can work to bring people together and dismantle inequality in all its forms.

I believe that asking the right questions is the key to finding the right answers, that passion is not a substitute for hard work, and that without empathy, technology is just a band-aid to the world’s problems rather than an effective solution.

I’m taking advantage of this year as an opportunity to explore problem spaces while I expand my skill sets. I hope you enjoy taking a look at what I’ve been up to so far!

-Ritu
